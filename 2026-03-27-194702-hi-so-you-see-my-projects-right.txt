Orator                                                                          
                                                                                
A modern, AI-powered reading app built with React Native and Expo. Orator helps 
 you manage and enjoy your digital library with intelligent content analysis,   
text-to-speech, and an accessible, comfortable reading experience.              
Features                                                                        
Multi-Format Support                                                            
                                                                                
    TXT (plain text)                                                            
    EPUB (standard e-book format)                                               
    PDF (AI-powered extraction with Google Gemini)                              
                                                                                
AI-Powered Content Analysis                                                     
                                                                                
    Automatic content type detection (books, articles, news, essays, stories,   
research, documents)                                                            
    Author and genre identification                                             
    Smart content summarization                                                 
    Suggested title improvements                                                
                                                                                
Reading Experience                                                              
                                                                                
    Clean, distraction-free reader                                              
    Sentence-by-sentence navigation                                             
    Multiple themes (dark, light, sepia, blue, custom)                          
    Adjustable font sizes (16px‚Äď24px)                                           
    Text-to-speech with playback controls                                       
    Progress tracking and bookmarking                                           
                                                                                
Library Management                                                              
                                                                                
    Organize content by type (Books, Articles, News, Essays, Stories, Research, 
 Documents)                                                                     
    Edit titles and metadata                                                    
    Delete unwanted content                                                     
    Track reading progress                                                      
    View content summaries                                                      
                                                                                
Smart Notifications                                                             
                                                                                
    Optional reading reminders                                                  
    Customizable reminder time
